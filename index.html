About Me

I'm Arjun, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast currently in my pre-final year at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, with a deep interest in uncovering and mitigating digital threats. I actively participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions and explore platforms like TryHackMe and Hack The Box to sharpen my skills. I enjoy working in Linux environments and using a wide range of cybersecurity tools to perform ethical hacking, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing. Continuously learning and evolving, I aim to make meaningful contributions to the cybersecurity world.
